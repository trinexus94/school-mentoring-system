About Us

Mentor&monitor is a system designed to not only monitor student perfomance but be a portal through which we can analyze the perfomance and use it gainfully into helping the student become better and do better in the consequent semesters.

Through this system, students can rate the coursework continuously throughout the semester thus ensuring continued feedback to the lecturers. The aom of this feature is to give the lecturer a good view of the students thoughts thus ensuring they are well informed as they adjust their teaching style.

The system gives the lecturer an entire page from which they can get pie charts giving them a summary of various aspects such as perfomance in a semester or how many students are registered for mentoring.
